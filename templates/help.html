Ultimate Movie Vendor is an application where users can sign in and submit their DVDs they have for sale.

Users can also add reviews for a DVD and these will be stored in the database and can help catalogue their collection.

The collection of reviews can be used for movie clubs in which people can trade ratings and experiences with a movie.
